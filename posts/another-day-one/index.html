Published August 18, 2019 Updated February 24, 2025 One-minute read

Day 1 Again Link to heading

I’ve had dozens of iterations of my blog. I’ve even had multiple active blogs for different topics, at the same time. My favorite blogging experience was when my wife was also actively blogging and we had a friendly competition. I’m going to try again. This time, I’m only going to keep one blog for all of the things. I have a short list of outcomes I trying to achieve:

  • Keep track of interesting findings
  • Practice my writing and content delivery
  • Practice and document a path to production

This time, I’m using Hugo and publishing directly to GitHub Pages. My decision to choose Hugo was heavily influenced by Robert Sirchia, James Williams, and Anton Kuzmenko.
